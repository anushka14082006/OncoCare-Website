Frequently Asked Questions

Home  |  FAQ
What types of cancer do you treat?
We treat a wide range including breast, lung, colon, prostate, blood cancers, and more with specialized care.
Do I need a referral to get an appointment?
No, you can book an appointment directly with us online or over the phone without any referral.
What is the cost of treatment?
Treatment costs vary based on type, stage, and duration. We offer transparent pricing after consultation.
Are your services covered by insurance?
Yes, we work with most major insurance providers. Our billing team can assist with claim processing.
Is genetic counseling available?
Yes, we provide genetic counseling and testing to identify hereditary risks and guide preventive care.
Can I get a second opinion from your team?
Absolutely. We welcome second opinion consultations and provide comprehensive diagnostic reviews.
Do you offer teleconsultation?
Yes, virtual consultations are available for new and follow-up patients via secure video conferencing.
